Data Deletion Policy – BladSense AI

BladSense AI respects your privacy and gives you full control over your personal and medical data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data and what happens when your data is removed.

1. Your Right to Delete Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Medical data (ultrasound images, bladder volume results)
  • App usage data and stored records
  • Uploaded ultrasound images

3. Option 1: Delete via the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the BladSense AI app.
  2. Go to Settings / Privacy.
  3. Tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the action.

Result: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

5. Data Retention

After deletion, your data is removed from active systems. Minimal logs may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes.

6. Security of Deletion
